Privacy Policy

Last updated June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Qui Ambassador app collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. The app is provided to authorized QUI Tequila brand ambassadors to manage in-store tasting events.

Who This Applies To

The Qui Ambassador app is for QUI Tequila brand ambassadors and people applying to become one. It is not intended for anyone under the legal drinking age. By using the app you confirm that you are of legal drinking age in your state (21+ in the United States). Full app access requires an application approved by QUI Tequila.

Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide and the information needed to run tasting events:

  • Account & profile: your name, email, phone number, home address, city, state, ZIP, preferred working days, and profile photo.
  • Registration applications: if you apply to join in the app, the application details you submit — name, phone, home address, preferred market and working days, and an optional photo. Applications are reviewed by QUI staff; if your application is not approved, we retain it (with the decision) so we can handle follow-ups and re-applications, and delete it on request.
  • Tasting events: store, market, chain, dates and times, starting and ending inventory, bottles sold, samples poured, kit details, comments, and the store manager’s name, email and signature.
  • Photos & media: tasting-table photos, expense receipts, route-proof screenshots, and signatures you capture.
  • Location: with your permission, your device’s location when you start a tasting and to get directions to stores.
  • Expenses & activities: amounts, reasons, durations, and supporting images.
  • Earnings: pay, bonus and reimbursement records.
  • Server logs: when the app contacts our servers we record the request, your IP address and your app version, solely to operate the service and keep it secure. We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not build usage profiles.

We do not collect bank or payment-card details inside the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Schedule and run your tasting events.
  • Verify attendance and calculate estimated compensation, bonuses and reimbursements.
  • Show your performance, earnings, streaks and milestones.
  • Process your expense and activity submissions.
  • Communicate with you and provide support.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the app.

We do not sell your personal information.

Location & Camera Permissions

The app requests access to your location and camera only for specific features:

  • Location records where a tasting takes place and opens directions to stores. You can decline or revoke location access in your device settings; some verification features may not work without it.
  • Camera and photo access let you capture receipts, tasting photos and signatures. You control when a photo is taken or selected.

How Your Information Is Shared

We share your information only as needed to run the program:

  • With QUI Tequila administrators and managers, who review tasting events, approve expenses and manage compensation.
  • With service providers that power the app — including Google Firebase (sign-in) and Google Cloud, Sheets and Drive (data and file storage) — under their security and privacy terms.
  • When required by law, or to protect rights, safety and the integrity of the program.

When you open external links from the app (the website, training video, quiz, or direct-deposit form), those services have their own privacy policies and handle any information you give them directly.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored on Google’s cloud infrastructure and sent over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. Some information — such as your current tasting session and preferences — is stored on your device. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as you are an active ambassador and as needed to administer the program, calculate pay, meet legal and tax obligations, and resolve disputes. You can request deletion as described below, subject to records we are required to keep.

Your Rights & Choices

You can:

  • Review and update your profile in the app.
  • Control location and camera permissions in your device settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal information from the app’s More tab.
  • Request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws.

Account & Data Deletion

Ambassador accounts are created when QUI Tequila approves your in-app application (or provisions you directly). You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data from inside the app: open the More tab and tap Delete Account. You can also use Contact Support in the More tab, or email us at support@quitequila.com. Pending or rejected applications can likewise be deleted on request.

We will delete your personal information — your profile, tasting recaps, expenses and activities — within 30 days of the request, except records we are legally required to retain (for example, pay and tax records). Any pay you are owed is settled before your account is closed, and we will email you once deletion is complete.

Age Requirement

This app is intended only for ambassadors of legal drinking age in their state (21+ in the United States). It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the legal drinking age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above, and significant changes may be communicated through the app.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your information? Reach us through Contact Support in the More tab, or at the contact details listed on quitequila.tribecaspirits.com.
